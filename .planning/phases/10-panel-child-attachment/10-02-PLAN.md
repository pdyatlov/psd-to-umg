<objective>
Extend the Generate-path refactor from Plan 10-01 into the reimport (Update) code path. `UpdateCanvas` currently hard-casts every nested parent to `UCanvasPanel` (line ~494, 500) and calls `AddChildToCanvas` for new children (line ~455). This plan mirrors the dispatch logic and adopts a clear-and-rebuild strategy (D-11) for non-canvas panels, since their slots carry no PSD-sourced state worth preserving.

Purpose: Without this plan, a user who reimports a PSD containing `@vbox`/`@hbox`/`@scrollbox`/`@overlay` groups would hit the same silent-drop bug fixed in 10-01, just in the reimport path. Keep Generate and Update behaviour symmetric.

Output: `UpdateCanvas` accepts `UPanelWidget*`, dispatches attachment on panel type, and for non-canvas panels with changed children uses the clear-and-rebuild strategy (call `Parent->ClearChildren()` then `PopulateChildren` from 10-01).
</objective>

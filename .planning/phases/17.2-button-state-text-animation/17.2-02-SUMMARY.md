- `FWidgetBlueprintGenerator::PopulateChildren` now skips `@state:*` layers when `Cast<UButton>(Parent) != nullptr`, positioned correctly after `@background` skip and before `zero-size` skip. Guard is scoped to UButton only — non-button panels with `@state:*` children are unaffected.
- `FButtonStateTextAnimSpec.cpp` flipped: both BTN-ANIM-01 `#if 0` guards became `#if 1`; BTN-ANIM-02 `bShouldSkip = false` replaced with live PopulateChildren predicate. Fourth It (full-generator) stays RED with Plan 03 sentinel.
